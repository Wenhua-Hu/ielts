雅思口语 - 2026年1月

1. Could you tell me about your hometown?
Answer: I come from a coastal city in China called Qingdao. It's famous for its beautiful beaches and beer. The city environment is very comfortable, and there are many tourists in the summer. I grew up there and have deep feelings for it.
2. What do you like to do in your free time?
Answer: In my free time, I enjoy reading and exercising. I usually go to the gym two or three times a week, and I also play basketball with friends. For reading, I mainly read novels and some popular science books, which help me relax.
3. Do you prefer working alone or with others?
Answer: It depends on the specific type of work. For tasks that require deep thinking, I prefer working alone so I can focus better. But for projects that need creativity and teamwork, I like working with others because different people have different ideas and can inspire each other.
4. Describe a book you have read recently.
Answer: Recently I read a novel called "To Live" by Yu Hua. It tells the story of a farmer who continues to survive strong after experiencing various hardships in life. This book made me think deeply about the meaning of life and made me cherish my current life more.
5. What kind of music do you like?
Answer: I enjoy listening to various types of music, but my favorites are pop music and classical music. Pop music makes me feel relaxed and happy, while classical music helps me calm down, especially when studying or working. I occasionally listen to jazz as well, which I find very flavorful.
